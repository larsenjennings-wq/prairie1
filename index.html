What We Do

Dirt Work Services in Shreveport, LA

If it involves moving, shaping, or placing dirt in Northwest Louisiana, we can handle it. Here's what we do most.

House Pads

Building a new home? We'll cut and grade a solid, level house pad — sized and sloped right from the start so your foundation contractor has a clean, ready surface.

Land Grading

Uneven land causes drainage problems, flooding, and wasted space. We grade lots, pastures, and yards so water moves where it's supposed to and your land lays flat.

Site Preparation

Before any structure goes up, the site has to be ready. We clear, cut, fill, and grade to get your property prepared for construction — whether it's a home, shop, or commercial build.

Fill Dirt Delivery & Placement

Need to raise a low spot or fill a large area? We deliver clean fill dirt and place it where it needs to go — spread and compacted, not just dumped in a pile.

Driveway Preparation

A properly prepared driveway base is the difference between gravel that lasts and gravel that turns into a mudhole. We cut, grade, and prep the base before your gravel or asphalt goes down.

Dirt Moving & Excavation

Sometimes you just need dirt moved from point A to point B. Ponds, ditches, trenches, clearing low spots, building up high spots — we run the iron and get it done.

Not sure if your project fits? Give us a call and we'll tell you straight.
